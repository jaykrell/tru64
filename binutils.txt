and place at the top of each:

#define PRIx64 "lx"

or see pyosf.h, i.e.:
/* C99 support is incomplete. */
#if defined(__osf__) && defined(__alpha__) && defined(__arch64__)
#define PRId64 "ld"
#define PRIi64 "li"
#define PRIo64 "lo"
#define PRIu64 "lu"
#define PRIx64 "lx"
#endif

Those are lowercase L for long.
Double lowercase ll also works instead of single lowercase l
